Incubation Model

I2india only takes on projects where it can add significant value in addition to investments. All entrepreneurs are subject to an intensive selection process. Selected entrepreneurs/teams are offered physical and managerial incubation support, in addition to financial investments. I2india takes an investment position in return for such support.

Our typical investments are in the range of Rs 15-25 lakhs at the seed stage, with effort from our team that will be worth several times the financial capital. We also assist in syndicating the Series A round of funding, with other investors. In return, we typically take 12% equity in the incubatees in the seed stage, with an aim to increase our holding over subsequent rounds of funding. Since i2india wins only if the entrepreneur does, we follow a very stringent and Darwinian process for supporting promising companies. Entrepreneurs interested only in financial support are strongly encouraged NOT to apply.

i2india, through its non-profit subsidiary, Technovate – runs a physical incubator in the heart of Bangalore. This center is equipped with all infrastructural and business services required to provide a plug-n-play environment for startups. Technovate’s unique business model has been officially recognized by the Government of India and is an approved Technology Business Incubator.
