About Us

Product startups in India follow a different path compared to Indian service startups and Western product startups. The Indian start-up eco-system mainly supports services companies. I2india has evolved a unique Indian model for product acceleration to power concept stage startups.

We work with Entrepreneurs interested in leveraging technology to build world-class products. Our mission is to fill the gap of product innovation in India.
