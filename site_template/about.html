About i2india

i2india was created on foundational investments by Imperial Innovations Plc UK (The Technology Transfer arm of Imperial College, London), CMG Partners UK and various leading Indian industry leaders and experts such as Tata Sons. Imperial Innovations UK is a $500 Million company that has commercialized more than 100 technologies over the past 20+ years.

i2india combines the activities of market research, company incubation, investment and intensive mentoring. The company is led by a team of Entrepreneurial Managers who have hands-on experience in product startups and who roll-up their sleeves to get involved.

The Conventional Approach





The i2india Approach



I2india has evolved two unique models for filling in local eco-system gaps:

Go-To-Market Strategy: B2B2C Model

This model addresses the biggest challenge of marketing costs/effort faced by product startups. I2india leverages its extensive personal and corporate network to develop intimate links between its incubatee companies and partner Brands or Businesses. The business plan is developed right from its initial stages, with such synergies in mind. The model guarantees a big-splash market entry with modest costs.

Intensive Mentoring: Co-Promotion Model

First generation entrepreneurs need more and experiential advice than high-level “gyan”. Unlike most mentors who have limited startup experience themselves, i2india mentors bring personal hands-on experience and near full-time involvement. We think of ourselves as Co-Promoters and our success is 100% dependent on that of our incubatees.
